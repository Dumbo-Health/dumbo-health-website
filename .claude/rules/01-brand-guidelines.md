# DumboHealth Brand Guidelines

## Brand Essence
- **Tagline**: "Breathe Easy, Live Fully."
- **North Star**: Empower better nights for brighter days.
- **Domain**: dumbo.health
- **Contact**: hello@dumbohealth.com | 305.302.5865

## Tone of Voice
- Warm & Expert, Never Distant
- Clear, Direct & Comforting
- Encouraging & Empowering
- Speak like a caring coach, not a technician
- Stigma-free, friendly, approachable language
- Medical credibility with human warmth

## Color Palette

| Name | Hex | CSS Variable | Proportion |
|------|-----|-------------|------------|
| **Daylight** | `#FCF6ED` | `--daylight` | 25% (background) |
| **Sunlight** | `#F5E6D1` | `--sunlight` | 25% (background) |
| **Light Peach** | `#FFD6AD` | `--light-peach` | 15% (warm accent) |
| **Peach** | `#FF8361` | `--peach` | 15% (CTA/accent) |
| **Teal** | `#78BFBC` | `--teal` | 10% (secondary accent) |
| **Midnight** | `#031F3D` | `--midnight` | 10% (text/headers) |

### Usage Rules
- Daylight + Sunlight = dominant backgrounds (50%)
- Light Peach + Peach = warm accents/CTAs (30%)
- Teal = secondary accent, highlights (10%)
- Midnight = text, headers, dark elements (10%)
- Black `#000000` and White `#FFFFFF` for high-contrast variants

## Typography

| Role | Typeface | Weights | Size Reference |
|------|----------|---------|----------------|
| **Headings** | **Nohemi** | Regular (400), Medium (500) | 56px hero, 40px h2, 28px h3 |
| **Body** | **Aeonik** | Regular (400), Bold (700) | 18px body-lg, 16px body |
| **Tags/Labels** | **Aeonik Mono** | Regular (400) | 14px tags |

### Tailwind Font Classes
- `font-heading` → Nohemi
- `font-body` → Aeonik
- `font-mono` → Aeonik Mono

## shadcn/ui Theme Mapping
- `--background` → Daylight (#FCF6ED)
- `--foreground` → Midnight (#031F3D)
- `--primary` → Peach (#FF8361)
- `--secondary` → Teal (#78BFBC)
- `--accent` → Light Peach (#FFD6AD)
- `--muted` → Sunlight (#F5E6D1)

## Design Principles
- Design-forward and stigma-busting
- Wellness meets clinical (not cold/hospital-like)
- Modern wellness brand feel (think Hims for sleep apnea)
- Vibrant colors, approachable language, tasteful humor
- Inclusive: women, younger adults, health enthusiasts
- Telehealth convenience with human warmth
