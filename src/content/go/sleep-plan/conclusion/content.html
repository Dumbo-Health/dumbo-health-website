There's no shame in needing more help. In fact, recognizing when you need it is a sign of wisdom, not weakness.

Consider scheduling a consultation with a Dumbo Health sleep specialist. They can look at your unique patterns, understand what's getting in the way, and create a personalized plan that goes deeper than any 30-day program can.

If you're sleeping better: That's beautiful. Really. You earned this. The consistency you built, the habits you kept, the nights you got up instead of lying there frustrated, it all mattered.

Now your job is to protect what you've built. Keep showing up for your core habits: your consistent wake time, your wind-down routine, keeping your bed sacred for sleep. Use your "bad night plan" when you need it, because those nights will still come sometimes.

That's not failure. That's life.
