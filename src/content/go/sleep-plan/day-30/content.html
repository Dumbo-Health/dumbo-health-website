How to Make This Last

Download your certificate. Print it, save it, share it if you want. Let it be a tangible reminder of what you committed to and completed.

Tell someone. Share your progress with a friend, a partner, or even just yourself in a journal entry. Naming your accomplishment out loud makes it real.

Recommit with specificity. Don't just say "I'll keep this up." Choose the specific habits that made the biggest difference for you and write them down. "I will continue waking up at 7 AM every day to protect my sleep rhythm." "I will continue using the 15-minute rule when I can't fall asleep." Make it concrete.

Be gentle with yourself. There will be nights you skip your wind-down routine or mornings you sleep in. That doesn't erase 30 days of progress. One off night is just one night. Come back to your habits the next day without guilt or drama.
