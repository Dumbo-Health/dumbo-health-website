### Best Practices
- Every page must have exactly one H1
- H1 should contain the primary keyword for that page
- H2s should cover subtopic keywords
- Maintain logical hierarchy (H1 > H2 > H3, never skip levels)

## 7. Internal Linking Strategy

### Current State
- Navigation provides basic cross-linking
- Blog articles likely link back to service pages (not fully audited per post)
- FAQ pages link to relevant services

### Recommendations
1. **Blog-to-service linking:** Every blog post should link to at least one service page (/at-home-sleep-test, /solutions, /pricing)
2. **Related posts widget:** Add related articles section at bottom of blog posts
3. **Pillar-cluster model:** Create pillar pages (comprehensive guides) with cluster posts linking back
4. **Contextual linking:** Add in-content links between related blog posts
5. **Service-to-blog linking:** Service pages should link to relevant educational blog content
6. **CTA consistency:** Every page should have a clear CTA linking to /at-home-sleep-test or signup

### Suggested Link Architecture
```
Homepage
  -> /solutions (primary service)
  -> /at-home-sleep-test (key conversion page)
  -> /pricing (decision page)
  -> /blog (educational hub)
       -> Individual posts -> back to /solutions, /at-home-sleep-test
  -> /about-us (trust/E-E-A-T)
  -> /faqs (support/trust)
```

## 8. Content Quality Signals

### Positive Signals
- Content is well-written and medically accurate
- Clear value proposition ("$149 at-home sleep test")
- Comparison positioning (Dumbo vs Traditional sleep care)
- Trust badges (HIPAA, FDA-regulated)
- Multi-state availability clearly communicated

### Areas for Improvement
- Add reading time estimates to blog posts
- Add published/updated dates to blog posts (visible)
- Add table of contents to long-form articles
- Improve content scannability with more bullet points and visual breaks
- Add video content (currently one YouTube embed on homepage)
- Consider adding a glossary page for sleep apnea terminology
