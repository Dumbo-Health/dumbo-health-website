## Full Content Summary

### Section 1: Introduction
Dumbo Health, Inc. ("Dumbo Health," "we," or "us") respects privacy and is committed to protecting it. The company is NOT a medical group or health care provider — it provides a platform connecting users to independent medical practices, specifically "Selene Medical Group, P.A."

**Personal Information definition:** Any information that identifies, relates to, describes, references, or could reasonably be linked with an individual or device.

**Applies to:** Platform usage, phone/telehealth visits, wearable device connections, electronic communications.
**Does NOT apply to:** Offline collection, employment data, third-party websites, HIPAA-governed platform areas.

Incorporated into Terms of Use.

### Section 2: No Children Under 13
Platform not intended for individuals under 13. No knowingly collected data from children.
Contact: privacy@dumbo.health

### Section 3: Information Collection
**Sources:** Direct (forms, calls, emails), automatic (cookies, tracking), payment processing (Stripe), service providers (Itamar, Resmed, Healthie, Somnoware, Impilo, MaskFit AR, Shopify, Supabase, AWS), third parties/healthcare providers.

**Tracking technologies:** Cookies (session + persistent), Google Ads/Analytics, Facebook Pixels, Hubspot, Instagram, LinkedIn, Mixpanel, Pinterest, Twitter, Customer.io

**Categories collected:**
- Identifiers (name, address, phone, email, SSN, driver's license, passport)
- Financial data (bank accounts, credit/debit cards)
- Medical/health info (insurance, biometric, sleep data, exercise data)
- Usage data (transactions, surveys, search queries, communications)
