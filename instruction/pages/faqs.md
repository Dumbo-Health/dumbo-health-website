## All FAQ Items (Complete)

### Testing & Preparation

1. **Q:** "How do I prepare for the home sleep test?"
   **A:** "Preparation is simple. Avoid caffeine and alcohol before bedtime, and follow the instructions provided with your test kit. The device is designed for ease of use, ensuring a comfortable testing experience."

2. **Q:** "What is a home sleep test, and how does it work?"
   **A:** "A home sleep test is a convenient way to assess for sleep apnea from the comfort of your own bed. You'll receive a FDA-approved small device that monitors your breathing, oxygen levels, and heart rate overnight. After the test, our sleep specialists analyze the data to provide an accurate diagnosis and tell you if you have sleep apnea. No need to visit any doctors or any sleep clinic, all of this from the comfort of your home"

3. **Q:** "When will I receive my results?"
   **A:** "Our team typically analyzes the data and provides results within a few days (most results within 48h). We'll then discuss the findings and next steps with you."

4. **Q:** "Is a home sleep test as accurate as an in-lab study?"
   **A:** "For many individuals, especially those with a high likelihood of obstructive sleep apnea, home sleep tests offer reliable results. However, certain complex cases might require an in-lab study for comprehensive evaluation. We will of course let you know if it's the case."

5. **Q:** "How does the testing process work?"
   **A:** "After completing a sleep questionnaire, our team ships the Home Sleep test to your home in about three business days. Setup is simple: pair the device with your phone, put it on your finger or hand, and keep it on all night."

6. **Q:** "What is an At-home sleep test?"
   **A:** "A small finger-worn device tracks how you breathe, how your heart beats, how much oxygen you have, and how long you sleep. It uses a light sensor on your finger and connects to an app on your phone. All the data is combined into one score that shows how many times your breathing slows or stops each hour."

7. **Q:** "Can this home sleep test really find sleep apnea?"
   **A:** "Yes. It's FDA-cleared and built to measure the signals needed to diagnose sleep apnea. It's been used on tens of thousands of people with strong accuracy."

8. **Q:** "Do I ever need a sleep lab?"
   **A:** "Almost never. Fewer than one percent of people need a full lab test with many sensors. Most people get everything they need from the home test."

9. **Q:** "What if my home sleep test doesn't record properly?"
   **A:** "Don't worry! If your test data isn't sufficient, we'll promptly send you another kit at no extra charge. Our goal is to ensure you receive accurate results quickly and easily."

10. **Q:** "Why is testing important?"
    **A:** "When your throat narrows during sleep, your oxygen drops. This stresses your heart, brain, and the rest of your organs. Untreated sleep apnea is linked to diabetes, stroke, heart attack, daytime sleepiness, and loss of sexual energy."

### Sleep Apnea & Treatment

17. **Q:** "What is sleep apnea?"
    **A:** "Sleep apnea is a condition where breathing repeatedly stops and starts during sleep, leading to disrupted rest and potential health risks. Common symptoms include loud snoring, daytime fatigue, and morning headaches. Learn more by clicking here."

18. **Q:** "What are the treatment options for sleep apnea?"
    **A:** "Treatment varies based on severity. Options include lifestyle changes, Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) therapy, oral appliances, and, in some cases, surgery."

19. **Q:** "Can changes to my lifestyle help with sleep apnea?"
    **A:** "Absolutely! Making healthy changes like eating well, exercising regularly, and managing your weight can greatly help with sleep apnea. Dumbo Health makes these changes easier through personalized treatment, ongoing support from sleep coaches, regular check-ins from our doctors, and engagement programs backed by real data and sleep specialists."

20. **Q:** "What is a CPAP machine, and why would I need one?"
    **A:** "A CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) machine gently helps you breathe better during sleep by keeping your airway open. Dumbo Health offers easy-to-use machines delivered right to your door, ensuring you're comfortable every night."

21. **Q:** "Is a CPAP machine uncomfortable or loud?"
    **A:** "Not anymore! Dumbo Health provides the latest CPAP machines that are quiet, comfortable, and user-friendly. Plus, our sleep coaches help you find the perfect fit."

22. **Q:** "What is CPAP therapy?"
    **A:** "CPAP stands for continuous positive airway pressure. It is a common treatment for obstructive sleep apnea that helps keep your airway open while you sleep so breathing stays steady through the night."

    **A:** "Yes. CPAP machines are considered medical devices, so you can bring them on flights in addition to your carry-on. If you plan to use it during the flight, notify your airline in advance and ask about power options, or bring a travel battery. Always pack your CPAP in carry-on (not checked luggage), and bring distilled water or plan to buy some at your destination."

### Prescriptions

33. **Q:** "Do I need a prescription to start my treatment?"
    **A:** "Yes, you'll need a prescription for sleep apnea treatment. Great news: Dumbo Health can handle this right here on our website! Our doctors can prescribe everything you need, quickly and easily. If you prefer, we can also give you the prescription directly."

34. **Q:** "Why do I need to upload a prescription?"
    **A:** "Sleep apnea treatments like CPAP machines and masks are regulated medical devices. A valid prescription is required before we can process your order or ship any equipment."

35. **Q:** "What type of prescription is accepted?"
    **A:** "Any prescription written by a licensed healthcare provider that clearly states your need for sleep apnea therapy. It can come from an in-lab sleep study or an at-home sleep test interpreted by a qualified physician."

36. **Q:** "How do I upload my prescription?"
    **A:** "Use the secure upload form. Take a clear photo or scan of your prescription and submit it along with your order ID."

37. **Q:** "Does the prescription need to include specific settings?"
    **A:** "Yes. If you're ordering a CPAP or APAP device, the prescription should include your pressure settings or a note allowing auto-titration. If settings are missing, your provider may need to update the prescription."

38. **Q:** "Can I use an old prescription?"
    **A:** "Most prescriptions for sleep apnea therapy do not expire, but some providers set time limits. If your prescription is unclear or outdated, you may be asked to provide a new one."

39. **Q:** "What if I lost my prescription?"
    **A:** "Contact the doctor who diagnosed your sleep apnea or interpreted your home sleep study. They can issue a copy or write a new prescription."

40. **Q:** "Can Dumbo Health write my prescription?"
    **A:** "If you complete a sleep evaluation through Dumbo Health and receive a diagnosis, our board-certified sleep physicians can write or update your prescription."

41. **Q:** "Is my information secure when I upload it?"
    **A:** "Yes. Uploads are encrypted and stored securely. Only authorized clinical staff can access your file."

42. **Q:** "How long does it take to verify my prescription?"
    **A:** "Most prescriptions are reviewed within one business day. If anything is missing or unclear, we'll notify you."

43. **Q:** "What happens after my prescription is approved?"
    **A:** "Your order moves into processing. If you're ordering a device, it will be set up with your prescribed settings before shipping."

44. **Q:** "Do I need a prescription to reorder supplies like masks or tubing?"
    **A:** "No. Most CPAP accessories do not require a prescription. Only the device itself and certain mask types do."

45. **Q:** "Can I upload my prescription from outside the United States?"
    **A:** "No. For regulatory reasons, Dumbo Health can only accept prescriptions written by licensed U.S. providers."

46. **Q:** "What if I still haven't completed a sleep test?"
    **A:** "You must complete a sleep study and receive a diagnosis before a prescription can be issued. Dumbo Health offers at-home sleep testing if you need one."

47. **Q:** "What makes a prescription invalid?"
    **A:** "Blurry images, missing provider details, missing pressure settings for CPAP/APAP, no indication of therapy type, prescriptions from non-U.S. providers. If invalid, you'll be asked to upload a corrected version."

48. **Q:** "Do I need a prescription for CPAP?"
